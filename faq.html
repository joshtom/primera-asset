Frequently asked Questions

Get answers to most pressing questions

How does it work?

At Primera Asset Management, you send your investment to the provided investment account and then you forward your proof of payments as well as your full name to the account manager attending to you, after which You’ll be given a new investors form (for new investors), a confirmation receipt and a memorandum of understanding. The confirmation receipt and memorandum of understanding serve as evidence of transaction and more importantly they affirm that we are indebted to you for the stipulated period of time on the documents. At the end of the investment period we request for your account details for your refund plus interest. You also have the option of re-investing. Our investors are usually happy with our services so they usually reinvest

What do you do with my money?

Funds pulled from various investors are used to trade the foreign exchange market which is the biggest and most liquid financial market in the world. Funds also go into real estate agriculture and energy.

What is the minimum and maximum amount I can invest?

The minimum investment amount at Primera Asset Management is €125.38 or it’s Naira equivalent while the maximum is unlimited

What is the guarantee that my investment is secure?

Your investment is secure because we have very stringent risk management procedures and also risk management with international brokerage firms.

How long does it take for me to get my ROI ( Return on Investment)

It depends on your preferred investment plan. If you opt for the monthly plan you get your ROI after a month. If you opt for the compound interest plan you get your ROI after the set period.

Can I withdraw the total money I invested?

Yes, your money can be withdrawn whenever you decide to discontinue with the investment plan.

In the case of an emergency, can I have access to my funds before my withdrawal date?

Yes you can make an emergency withdrawal within 24 to 72 hours but you would lose all interest accrued.

What happens when you make losses?

Losses are usually inevitable in every business but at Primera Asset Management the losses are usually minimal and very insignificant compared to our profits. Also we have structures and strategies in place to effectively manage risks

As an open-ended ended investment company, we allow investors to invest and withdraw capital periodically based on the fund's net asset value.
